What is BitDegree?

BitDegree - an educational platform connecting Tech students, Sponsors & Digital service providers fueled by Ethereum based token economy.
Helping ordinary people to study, build & design amazing projects for free. Earn token scholarships and status. Get linked with world-changing companies & launch new career.

It is a new Coursera & HackerRank combined together and powered by blockchain technology and is the only future alternative to the absolutely crippled conventional education. BitDegree gives people free opportunities that would they never have if they had to rely on the traditional education model.

Developed on a top of 29+ millions solid-based Hostinger & 000webhost users ready for a change. It is privately funded and backed by companies like Hostinger International.


BitDegree is building education the world really in need by reaching people from all over the globe & giving them interactive tools to learn and practice new technologies with the Ethereum blockchain powered scholarship system that inspire them to keep on learning. It is the sourcing network that uncovers hidden engineering talents and bridges them to the world-changing companies.

Why BitDegree?

1. Global education is broken

Universities and higher education institutions are universally failing to fulfill their real-world mission - to teach know-how to their students that would guarantee employment. The entire education system globally is stagnant and uses centuries old methods that are not effective. There is a need for paradigm shift at the global level to disrupt the status quo and the blockchain is one of the answers.

2. Shortage of hi-tech employees

There is a huge shortage of qualified hi-tech employees all around the globe. A key success to any tech company is talent acquisition. Leading companies are willing to invest & attract the best industry talents. Employer branding for millions of tech students is the solution. By sponsoring token scholarships on a global or local level, young talents are spotted early and bridged with the companies that are in constant need.

3. Tech students as a valued customers

With a traditional client acquisition model, tech companies spend a huge amount of budget to acquire a new customer. By accepting decentralized BitDegree tokens, they will be able to attract new flow of technophile customers in a cost-effective way. Students who earned scholarships are qualified customers with a proven interest in learning technology & are willing to spend on products & services.

How it works

Introducing a decentralized, transparent education platform with token scholarships & talent networking based on Ethereum Blockchain & self-sustaining Token economy.

1. Companies as Sponsors

Sponsors - leading companies, they are the main fuel for self-sustainable BitDegree token economy. For them, the economic incentive of buying tokens is to do employer branding and to get into the bridge with students network to recruit IT talents on demand. Invested tokens are distributed to tech students as micro-scholarships & rewards or free sponsored courses. Sponsors purchase BitDegree tokens and with tokens also fund project reviewers, mentors and expert coaches of creations to deliver the hi-tech courses related to their business area with an incentive to link into the skilled developer sourcing system and recruit talents, to scale engineering teams cost-effectively & to commit branding. Learning Foundation platform allows to reach more diverse & a wider pool of talents, as well as enhancing the whole screening process.

2. Students as Consumers

Students - consume the industry most efficient courses by mastering fundamentals or even advance their future career knowledge. They achieve the most competitive skills according to world's most forward–thinking companies - Facebook, Google, IBM, GitHub, Tesla, Hostinger and more. In addition to educating themselves, the students are incentivized by any of the following: micro-scholarships, rewards upon completion of courses or exams, prizes and competitions, all of which are provided in BitDegree tokens. All achievements and BitDegree Diplomas are safely locked in the blockchain, verified and are traceable by unique Student Crypto-ID. Students are self-motivated to perform best and are open to an amazing career opportunities to get into the Talents network and to be evolved in hiring processes with partnered industry Sponsors in their area.

3. Digital Service Providers

Digital Services Providers - third parties will opt-in to exchange or accept BitDegree tokens as a mean of discount or voucher and in this way to enable the students to receive digital goods or services (i.e. cloud services, domains, gadgets, web hosting, VPN services, software and electronics, SAAS, ADs exchange). Tokens will perform as an extra acquisition channel and an additional cost-effective advantage in market competition. Decentralized by removing middle-firms it will enable digital third party service providers and students to transact with each other without any intermediaries. Normally from perspective of students role, BitDegree tokens can be spent on third party service providers converting them into physical goods or digital services, on learning foundation premium courses, liquidated in exchange, kept as long-term token investment or as an instant trade.

FAQs

What is BitDegree Token?

Based on Ethereum technology, BitDegree Token is a utility that can be used as an account unit between between sponsors, advertisers, students in a new blockchain-based learning platform. Note that the token is not a currency.

How can I buy Tokens?

Tokens can be bought with Smart Contracts. More information regarding Token purchases will be provided soon.

Where will Tokens be used?

The Token will be used on the BitDegree platform in order to obtain services from the platform and conduct translations on the platform. More information will be provided soon.

Are Tokens transferable? Where are Tokens traded?

The purpose of Learn Tokes is for it to be used on the BitDegree platform as an exchange unit. Yes, Tokens are transferable. The information regarding places where Tokens can be traded will be provided soon.

I transferred Token from one wallet to another & it never arrived.

We do not store or keep any information regarding wallet usage and unfortunately cannot help you directly. The best way in order to solve such problem is contacting the providers of the wallet that you are using.

Which wallets can be used to store Token?

Learn Token is an ERC20 token. In order to store the Learn Token(s) any wallet that is compatible with ERC20 can be used.

What do Tokens represent?

Tokens represent a utility used in order to use the BitDegree platform - a new blockchain technology-based learning platform. Token ownership means only the right to use them in order to obtain services from the BitDegree platform as well as enable the usage and interaction with the platform. Token ownership does not represent any stakes, shares, rights to future profits or any ownership of any intellectual property or any other form of participation related to the BitDegree platform.

Will I lose my Token after 6 mo.?

No, you will not use any Token after 6 month period, as the Learn tokes in built according to ERC20 token standards.
